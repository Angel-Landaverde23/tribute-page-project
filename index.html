FC Barcelona

About

FC Barcelona was founded on the 29th of November in 1899 by a group of young foreigners living in Barcelona.During the 1920s, football became a sport of the masses. FC Barcelona continued to attract huge amounts of support and was viewed as a dream team FC Barcelona managed to secure four consecutive Spanish League championships, between 1990 and 1994 and Winning the European Cup in 1992.From the season 2008/09 under Josep Guardiola, Guardiola was a firm supporter of basing his team around the club's own youth system and promoted several young talents to the first team, and the result was the greatest Barça team ever.This team was the culmination of everything that FC Barcelona stands for, and produced an amazing string of major titles, including two Champions Leagues and three Spanish Leagues, plus the long-awaited Clubs World Cup, which was finally won in 2009, that extraordinary year when Barcelona won all six major trophies, something unprecedented in the history of European football.Barça broke all kinds of records, played memorable matches and won just about every title on offer. But the finest hour for Guardiola's side came at Wembley on 28 May, 2011, when the world was enchanted by the kind of football that dreams are made of. The Guardiola era ended in 2012 but Barça continued their success under Tito Vilanova (the league in 2012/13) and Luis Enrique who in 2015 won the Champions League, the League and the Copa del Rey in another historic treble winning season.

Facts

They invented their own style of play called “tiki taka”.

Lionel Messi is the all-time top scorer for Barcelona with 672 goals in 778 matches played.

Barcelona fans are called culers,Blaugranes or Azulgranes.

Barcelona FC built their first stadium in 1909 with a capacity of 6,000 people.

Barcelona holds one of the world's biggest rivalries with Real Madrid.

Club-legends

Diego Armando Maradona - Maradona was known for his incredible dribbling skills, speed, and precise ball control, as well as his controversial off-field antics.

Ronaldo de Assís Moreira ‘Ronaldinho’ - Ronaldinho joined the Club in 2003 and changed its history forever. In his five seasons at the club he became one its greatest ever footballers. He was also known for his skills with the ball in the game and off the game.

Carles Puyol - He was a fierce competitor who put in everything he had each and every game.

Xavi Hernandes - is, statistically speaking, the most successful player in Spanish football history. A midfielder from the club’s youth system, he’s the perfect example of the Barça valuesp

Andrés Iniesta - Iniesta was one of the most loved and emblematic of players for the Barça fans. The ‘culers’ has always valued his silky style of play, his commitment to the team and his modesty and good humour off the pitch.

Sergio Busquets - Sergio Busquets from the third division to winning the sextuple in a single season showed from the very first day that he had what it took to be part of the greatest Barça team ever.

Leo Messi - Messi is the greatest player of all time and FC Barcelona record setter. Trained at La Masia, he will always be associated with Barça as the greatest player ever to wear the blaugrana shirt.
